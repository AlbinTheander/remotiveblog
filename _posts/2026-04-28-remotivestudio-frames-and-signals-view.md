RemotiveStudio's Tree view lets you explore RemotiveTopology platforms and instances. It also allows you to show all frames and signals in that platform. Now, these concepts are married together to include signal data directly in your topology tree view.

- **Expand any channel** to see all frames and signals transmitted on it
- **Expand any ECU** to see which frames it transmits and receives
- **View signal details** like type, range, and unit directly in the tree
- **Search and filter** to quickly find specific frames or signals

This makes it easier than ever to find your way around your platform!
